In this short survey and presentation, you will study a language and give brief presentation about its linguistic and other related facts, such as demographics, location, orthography, morphology, syntax, phonology, corpus, tools, and translation systems and performance. Please feel free to research on your own or consulting native speakers and/or language experts.
This project was inspired from Nizar Habash's original assignment. Here are some example resources for reference:
  • Example from Nizar Habash's class
  • WMT resources
  • Wikipedia
  • Omniglot
  • World Atlas of Language Structures online
You will prepare a few slides and present in one 10mins slot in class.
