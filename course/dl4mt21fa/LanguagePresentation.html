Language Survey and Presentation for DL4MT

In this short survey and presentation, you will study a language and give brief presentation about its linguistic and other related facts, including
  • how the text is composed and written in the language, its orthography, morphology, syntax;
  • how the language is spoken (some examples);
  • where is it spoken, its location and demographics;
  • gather corpus and tools, gather monolingual and bilingual data for the language;
  • Current machine translation system (if any) and their performance
Please feel free to research on your own or consulting native speakers and/or language experts.
This project was inspired from Nizar Habash's original assignment. Here are some example resources for reference:
  • Example from Nizar Habash's class
  • WMT resources
  • Wikipedia
  • Omniglot
  • World Atlas of Language Structures online
  • Common crawl: open crawl of web data
You will prepare a few slides and present in one 10mins slot in class.
