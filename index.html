My interest is to advance machine intelligence to better assist human in information creation, comprehension, and sharing. To this end, I develop machine learning and statistical technologies that learn from data and past experience. In particular, I work on natural language processing, machine translation, probabilistic inference, optimization algorithms, and data mining.
