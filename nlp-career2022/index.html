Diversity and Inclusion Workshop: How to Grow a Career in NLP?

Introduction

It is a half-day workshop & gathering event dedicated for developing a diverse career path in NLP. The objective is to provide a forum for successful leaders in various sectors to introduce experiences in career building, and to help young researchers and students to develop diverse career goals.
